    FBLA Quiz



    The quiz consists of 5 questions with 4 different types of questions (multiple choice, true/false, fill-in-the-blank, and dropdown). There is no time limit on the test. You will receive your score immediately after you submit the test, along with which questions you answered correctly and incorrectly and the amount of time you took on the test. You can also print out your test results. You can take the test again but keep in mind that the quiz will also be randomized each time, with questions being taken from a 50-question database.




    If you would like, you can see the list of questions which may be asked on the quiz. They are organized by four question types. There are 20 multiple choice questions, 10 true/false questions, 10 fill-in-the-blank questions, and 10 dropdown questions. You can also see the answer choices and the correct answer for each question. You can use this page to review your answers after your test.




    To navigate around the website, you can use the navbar at the top in order to get to different pages. There are also buttons on each page to get from one page to another.
